Our Story

How Bedo Transportation came to serve our community with reliable, elegant transportation solutions

From Humble Beginnings

Bedo Transportation was founded in 2015 with a single vehicle and a simple mission: to provide reliable, comfortable transportation with a personal touch. What began as a small operation serving local business professionals has grown into a trusted name for elegant transportation and essential courier services.

Our founder, Michael Bedo, recognized a need in the community for transportation that combined professionalism with genuine care for passengers. After years in the corporate world, he decided to create a service that would treat every client like family while maintaining the highest standards of quality.

Our Commitment to Excellence

At Bedo Transportation, we believe that every journey matters. Whether it's a bride on her wedding day, a patient receiving vital medication, or an executive heading to an important meeting, we approach each service with the same level of dedication and attention to detail.

Our decision to specialize in medical courier services came from seeing firsthand how challenging it can be for individuals to access their medications. We've implemented strict HIPAA-compliant procedures to ensure privacy and security while delivering these essential items.
