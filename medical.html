Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service HIPAA compliant?

Yes, all our medical courier staff complete HIPAA training and we maintain strict protocols to protect patient privacy. We handle all health information with the utmost confidentiality.

What types of medications can you transport?

We can transport most prescription medications, including controlled substances (with proper documentation). We have temperature-controlled storage for medications requiring refrigeration.

How quickly can you deliver?

We offer same-day delivery for requests received by 2pm. Emergency deliveries may be available outside standard hours - please call to discuss urgent needs.

Do you work directly with pharmacies?

Yes, we partner with several local pharmacies who arrange deliveries directly through us. We can also establish new pharmacy partnerships upon request.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the entire metro area and surrounding suburbs. Contact us with your location to confirm service availability.

How is payment handled?

Payment can be arranged through your pharmacy, billed to insurance in some cases, or paid directly by the patient. We accept all major credit cards for direct payments.
